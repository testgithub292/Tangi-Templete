Investing your way into the future

For investors, the question is always the same: Where’s the opportunity? What’s the edge? Why

At Tangi, we provide compliant banking and payment processing solutions to industries that major banks and card networks refuse to serve. Where You Fit In — And Why This Opportunity Exists

The Problem

Payment solutions in the cannabis industry have not failed because they lack innovation. They have failed because they lack one crucial element—a banking partner.

Despite the legal cannabis industry generating $33 billion in retail sales annually, major banks and payment networks like Visa and MasterCard refuse to support it. This has forced 8,000 retailers and 60 million consumers into an outdated, cash-only system—creating inefficiencies, security risks, and lost revenue.

Without a bank to support transactions, every payment provider before us has either collapsed or been forced into non-compliance, inevitably getting shut down. That’s why the market is still dominated by cash, despite its $33 billion annual turnover. Click Here To Learn More About The Problem

Our Solution

Our competitive advantage isn’t patentable technology or specialized hardware. It’s the one thing that no other provider has obtained and the one thing required for susseess in this sector: a banking partner. We have secured a banking relationship with a forward-thinking bank that shares our vision of providing compliant banking and payment services to the cannabis industry.

This exclusive banking relationship is our most valuable asset. It’s the foundation that allows our payment system to operate legally and sustainably, and it’s what gives our token the credibility and utility to thrive.

Our all-in-one payment terminal offers fully bank-backed payment options — including debit, credit, and direct bank-to-bank transfers — giving cannabis retailers the compliance, speed, and convenience they’ve long been denied. But the real innovation is Tangi Token. While traditional methods attract and onboard the customer, every road leads to token payments. Thanks to our exclusive banking partnership, we’re the only provider able to offer this fully compliant bridge — allowing us to easily capture the market of 60 million unbanked cannabis consumers and gradually transition them to the token, where the true value lies for retailers, consumers, and investors alike.

It positions Tangi not just to participate in the market, but to dominate it. Because our payment infrastructure is built on compliance, our token is now on a direct path to adoption among up to 60 million end users who need it as a tool for everyday transactions. 60 Million Potential Token End Users

Tangi’s Addressable Market Is Twice the Size of Binance’s User Base

With access to over 60 million cash-based consumers in the U.S. cannabis market, Tangi is uniquely positioned to serve a community double the size of Binance’s 30 million users — but with one major advantage: real-world use, not just speculation.

Consumers gravitate to our platform because, for the first time, they can pay like they do everywhere else — no cash, no clunky apps. Once inside our ecosystem, consumers aren't forced to change behavior overnight. We first capture them through traditional, familiar means — debit cards, credit cards, mobile payments — just like any other retail experience. From there, we begin to incentivize token use for payments through rewards, exclusive discounts, free vouchers, and loyalty bonuses. Everyone in the chain—consumers, retailers, and our company—is incentivized to adopt, promote, and use token payments. Click Here To See How

Once consumers experience the benefits of paying with Tangi Token, we introduce our auto-replenishment feature. Their wallets are topped up automatically based on their typical weekly or monthly spend. Their card or bank is simply charged for a token bundle that matches their historical usage. They tap their phone in-store to pay with Tangi Tokens, while our software automatically applies rewards and discounts in the background. It’s smooth, intuitive, and virtually invisible to the end user.


Here’s Where Things Get Powerful For Investors

From Storefront to Exchange — The Cycle That Fuels Investor Gains

And they’re doing it automatically, every week, every month, on a continuous loop. These are repeat buyers. They don’t even need to think about it. They just keep spending, earning, and reloading — driving token demand in a sustainable, compounding cycle.
