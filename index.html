What We’ve Built

Tangi provides compliant, bank-integrated payment processing for highly regulated industries where traditional providers refuse to operate.

Our flagship solution focuses on the legal cannabis market — a $30B+ U.S. industry with over 8,000 retailers and 60 million consumers that still rely heavily on cash due to regulatory limitations and banking restrictions.

At the heart of our system is Tangi Token (TNG) — a real utility token used to facilitate payments across the retail cannabis ecosystem.

With traditional banking partners unwilling to serve this sector, we've solved the single greatest challenge: we secured an exclusive partnership with a U.S.-regulated, pro-crypto bank.

This gives us — and only us — the ability to offer cannabis retailers a legal, transparent, fully compliant payment platform with traditional card payments, mobile payments, and a pathway to crypto-token adoption.

The Token with Real Utility
