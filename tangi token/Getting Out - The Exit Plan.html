Getting Out - The Exit Plan

From Investment to Returns

As an investor, your tokens will be positioned for high demand from two key buyer groups: new investors entering the market post-listing and end users who rely on the token for everyday transactions.

When we publicly list, we will attract a new wave of investors who will see a fully operational ecosystem, a compliant payment infrastructure, and a rapidly growing user base—creating strong upward pressure on token value.

Beyond investor interest, the token will be embedded within an essential payment system used by thousands of retailers and millions of consumers. These end users will not only adopt the token but depend on it for rewards, discounts, and lower transaction costs. As they transition from our closed-loop system to the exchange, they will become repeat buyers, driving sustained demand and liquidity.

As a Round 2 investor, you have the flexibility to capitalize on this demand—whether by selling to incoming investors at the time of listing or by holding as mass adoption unfolds, maximizing long-term value.
